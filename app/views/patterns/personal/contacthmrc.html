Contact HMRC




Context

This page was published in July 2019. It instructs readers how to inform Her Majesty’s Revenue and Custom (HMRC) about a change in address personal details; for example, when updating details after changing surname. The page can be accessed directly from the HMRC homepage. Most users access the page from a search engine.


Research and findings

This pattern was found to meet WCAG 2 on 06/06/2019. 16 user interviews were carried out to test the pattern at various stages in the research process using an agile model. One survey was published to gather user needs and had 106 responses. The pattern is due for review on 01/07/2022.
